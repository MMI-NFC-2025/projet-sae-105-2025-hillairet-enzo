The Forgotten Figures.

This book explores how history has often invisibilized women, whether they were scientists, artists, or political figures.

Through concrete examples and historical analyses, the author shows how their contributions were minimized or attributed to men.

In the context of our website, this work perfectly illustrates the Matilda Effect and the need to rehabilitate forgotten female scientists, like Cecilia Payne-Gaposchkin.

It helps complement the articles by providing a historical and critical perspective on the invisibilization of women in science and society.
