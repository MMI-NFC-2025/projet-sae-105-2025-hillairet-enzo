Glossary.

Explanation.

Matilda Effect.
Tendency to minimize or attribute to men discoveries made by female scientists.
Spectroscopy.
Scientific method involving analyzing the light emitted by stars to determine their chemical composition.
Astrophysics.
Branch of astronomy that studies the structure, composition, and evolution of celestial bodies.
Scientific Popularization.
Action of making complex notions accessible to all, in a simple and understandable way.
Scientific Recognition.
Process by which a discovery or research is officially attributed to a person or group of researchers.
Invisibilization.
Phenomenon by which individuals or groups (often women) are erased or ignored in historical or scientific narratives.
Timeline.
Succession of events in the order they occurred.
